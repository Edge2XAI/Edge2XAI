About Edge2XAI

Edge2XAI is an industry-led initiative focused on enabling universities and research groups to translate embedded systems and AI research into deployable Edge AI solutions.

We work closely with academic institutions to:

Our expertise spans:

  • TinyML & Edge AI
  • Low-Power, Secure, and Scalable AI Systems
